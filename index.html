About Me

Víctor Rubio Jornet is a PhD researcher in Network Engineering at the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC), Barcelona. His research seeks to study formal privacy guarantees in unstructured data such as trajectories and graphs. He is a recipient of the Joan Oró doctoral scholarship (AGAUR) and has held a research stay at the University of Edinburgh. His work draws on differential privacy, generative models, and privacy attacks to bridge the gap between theoretical privacy and its practical deployment.

Research Interests

My research focuses on protecting unstructured data for machine learning applications with formal privacy guarantees. I work to research into the balance between data utility and privacy, using: differential privacy, and synthetic data generation mainly in graphs and trajectories. Key topics include:


Differential Privacy Synthetic Data Generation Trajectory Privacy Graph Privacy Membership Inference Attacks Generative Models Machine Learning
